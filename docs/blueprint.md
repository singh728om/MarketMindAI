# **App Name**: MarketMind AI

## Core Features:

- AI Photoshoot Agent: Generates photoshoot prompts based on product details and desired styles, enhancing visual appeal for listings. Tool will use provided data about the product in making decisions about how to incorporate details like fabric and lighting. Integrates with DALL-E/Midjourney via mock API.
- AI Listing Optimizer: Optimizes product listings with AI-generated titles, bullets, and descriptions. Improves SEO and conversion rates on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra. Includes an SEO score feature.
- AI Video Ad Agent: Creates video ad storyboards and prompts for Runway/Pika. Automates video ad creation, making promotional content more engaging. Supports image-to-video functionality.
- AI Catalog Automation Agent: Generates and validates product catalog templates. Streamlines catalog management and ensures data consistency across marketplaces.
- UGC Script Studio: Generates hooks and scripts for UGC (user-generated content) campaigns to connect with audiences and leverage peer influence. Supports the generation of a creator brief.
- Weekly Client Report Generator: Generates automated client reports with narrative insights, graphs, and trends. It converts raw data into an engaging document.
- Brand Health Score: Estimates a brand health score (0-100) by evaluating customer reviews and various relevant metrics. Visualized in a dedicated dashboard.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, saturated indigo (#4F46E5) to evoke trust, authority, and sophistication, in alignment with a high-end agency.
- Background color: Very dark, desaturated indigo (#1A1A2E) to provide a modern, premium feel and ensure legibility in dark mode.
- Accent color: Analogous blue-violet (#7952B3) for highlights, interactive elements, and calls to action.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif known for its modern and neutral appearance.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif to provide a contemporary, scientific, clean and technical look.
- Lucide-react icons: consistent, modern icons throughout the app, providing visual cues for services and actions.
- Sidebar dashboard layout: intuitive navigation for key modules and agents, improving user accessibility.
- Framer Motion: Subtle micro-animations and transitions, enhancing UI interactivity and user experience.
